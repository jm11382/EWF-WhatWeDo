MORE CLEAN WATER FOR MADAGASCAR

The Elemental Water Foundation and local communities in Madagascar are collaborating to break the cycle of water scarcity by enabling sustainable access to affordable and safe drinking water, sold by and for the community, using only the energy provided by the sun.

Donate

What we do

Since 2017, Elemental Water Foundation has sought to improve the availability of clean and affordable water for people who need it the most. By providing access to fresh water through desalination of salt water, we strive to enable safe and affordable fresh water on a global scale for people that face water scarcity. We sincerely hope that you will join us in achieving our goal of improving the lives of those in these communities.

Building upon the experience and partnerships realized with our first Madagascar water project, we are certain that we can again make a difference and generate sustainable impact.
